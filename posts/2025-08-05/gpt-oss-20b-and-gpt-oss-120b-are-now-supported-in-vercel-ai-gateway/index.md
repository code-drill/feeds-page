🚀 Exciting news! Vercel AI Gateway now supports OpenAI's gpt-oss-20b and gpt-oss-120b models. These open-weight reasoning models enhance access with a unified API, simplifying usage tracking and performance optimizations. 💻 Developers can easily integrate them using AI SDK v5, with built-in observability and support for multiple model providers.
