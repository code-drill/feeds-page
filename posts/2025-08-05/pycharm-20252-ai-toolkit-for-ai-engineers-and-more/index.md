Discover the new features in PyCharm 2025.2! 🚀 This release introduces the AI Toolkit, designed to support AI engineers with tools for experimentation, debugging, and deployment all within the IDE. Additionally, it marks the last version of PyCharm Community, paving the way for a unified PyCharm experience. Notable updates include enhancements to AI Assistant, Junie, and notebooks.
