🚀 Exciting news for developers! OpenAI's open weight models are now available on AWS through Amazon Bedrock and Amazon SageMaker JumpStart. These models, gpt-oss-120b and gpt-oss-20b, are designed for text generation and reasoning tasks, providing flexibility and control over your projects. With a 128K context window and adjustable reasoning levels, they offer enhanced capabilities for coding, scientific analysis, and more.
