🚀 HashiCorp is transitioning to IBM starting September 1, 2025. This move aims to enhance customer value while maintaining the trust you've built with HashiCorp. 🔧 Key updates include changes to product naming conventions, aligning with IBM’s portfolio, but no alterations to features. Customers using legacy products will benefit from upgrades at current pricing. 💼 Billing operations will also shift to IBM, with new customer numbers issued. PAYG customers will see no changes.
