Now Live: GPT-5 on the DigitalOcean Gradient™ AI Platform

2025-08-07 23:00

🚀 Exciting news! GPT-5 is now live on the DigitalOcean Gradient™ AI Platform. Developers can access this advanced model via serverless inference APIs or the Gradient AI Platform SDK. You can also use your own OpenAI API key for integration. GPT-5 enhances reasoning and performance, making it ideal for tasks like financial planning, medical analysis, and creative content generation. Get started today! 🌟 #GPT5 #DigitalOcean #AIPlatform #Developers #OpenAI
