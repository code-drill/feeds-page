Vercel collaborates with OpenAI for GPT-5 launch

2025-08-07 13:00

🚀 Vercel has partnered with OpenAI to launch the GPT-5 family of models! These models are now available through AI Gateway and in production on v0.dev. Vercel has been testing GPT-5 in various environments, and the results indicate improved frontend design capabilities. GPT-5 generates clean, composable code and enhances workflows, particularly with long-context reasoning and tool handling. Vercel is already integrating GPT-5 into its dashboard and other applications. #Vercel #OpenAI #GPT5…
