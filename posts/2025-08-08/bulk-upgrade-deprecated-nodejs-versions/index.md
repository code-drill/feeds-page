🚀 Upgrade your Node.js projects easily! Team owners can now update all projects using Node.js 18 or earlier to Node.js 22 with just one click in the Vercel Dashboard. Remember, if your project specifies a version in package.json, you'll need to update it manually. Existing deployments remain unaffected. Check out your deprecated Node.js projects today! #Nodejs #Vercel #TechUpdate #WebDevelopment #SoftwareEngineering
