🚀 NVIDIA has announced the general availability of NVIDIA Isaac Sim 5.0 and NVIDIA Isaac Lab 2.2 at SIGGRAPH 2025. These frameworks are now accessible on GitHub, providing developers with tools for building, training, and testing AI-powered robots in physics-based simulations.
