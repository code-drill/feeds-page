📊 Exciting news for Tableau Cloud users! Tableau has introduced Custom Domains, allowing site admins to set up a brand-specific subdomain, like analytics.example.com. This feature simplifies access to published content and enhances brand identity. 🔒 Key benefits include improved security and seamless embedding of analytics, even in browsers that block third-party cookies. To get started, admins can configure Custom Domains in site settings at no extra cost. #Tableau #BusinessIntelligence...
