🇵🇭 FilBench - Can LLMs Understand and Generate Filipino?

2025-08-12 00:00

Exploring the capabilities of LLMs in Filipino languages is essential. 🇵🇭 FilBench, a new evaluation suite, assesses LLM fluency, translation, and cultural knowledge in Tagalog and Cebuano. Despite high ChatGPT usage in the Philippines, systematic evaluations are lacking. The study evaluates over 20 LLMs, aiming to provide clear insights. 📄 Read the paper: arxiv.org 🖥️ Check GitHub: github.com #FilBench #LLMs…
