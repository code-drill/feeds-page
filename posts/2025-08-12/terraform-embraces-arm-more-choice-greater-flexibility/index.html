Terraform embraces Arm: More choice, greater flexibility

2025-08-12 16:00

🚀 Terraform has expanded support for Arm architecture, giving customers more options for their IT investments. With the rise of Arm’s popularity for its cost savings and performance benefits, Terraform now allows running Terraform Enterprise and HCP Terraform agents on Arm-based platforms like AWS Graviton.
