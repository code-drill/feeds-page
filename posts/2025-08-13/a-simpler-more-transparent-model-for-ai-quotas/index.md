🚀 Exciting changes coming to JetBrains AI! Starting August 18, 2025, a new AI quota model will simplify subscriptions. Users can seamlessly extend usage beyond their quota with clear, real currency pricing. This update aims for transparency and better management of AI resources. Each subscription tier will align with its price, providing equivalent AI Credits. Additional credits can be purchased as needed. Stay tuned for more updates! #JetBrains #AI #TechUpdates #SubscriptionModel #Transparency
