Gemini CLI + VS Code: Native diffing and context-aware workflows

2025-08-13 00:00

🚀 Exciting news for developers! The latest update to Gemini CLI enhances its integration with VS Code. This update offers context-aware suggestions by accessing your workspace and selected text. This means targeted help right when you need it. Additionally, you can now use native in-editor diffing. Review and modify proposed changes directly in a full-screen diff view for better control over your code. 🔧 Ready to enhance your workflow? Check your Gemini CLI version and get started! #GeminiCLI…
