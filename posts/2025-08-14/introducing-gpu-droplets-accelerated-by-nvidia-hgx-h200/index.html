Introducing GPU Droplets accelerated by NVIDIA HGX H200

2025-08-14 14:00

🚀 Exciting news for AI developers! DigitalOcean has launched NVIDIA HGX H200 GPU Droplets, offering up to 2x faster inference speeds and double the memory capacity compared to the H100. These droplets are designed for simplicity and scalability, priced at just $3.44/GPU/hr. With 141 GB of memory and HBM3e technology, they enhance performance for demanding tasks in AI and HPC. All droplets are HIPAA-eligible and SOC 2 compliant, ensuring security. Start accelerating your AI projects today!…
