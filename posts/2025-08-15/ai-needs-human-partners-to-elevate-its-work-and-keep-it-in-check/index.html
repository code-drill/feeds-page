AI needs human partners to elevate its work – and keep it in check

2025-08-15 19:57

AI is transforming industries, especially in pharmaceutical innovation. 🧬 Researchers at MIT are using generative AI to design new antibiotics, potentially speeding up drug development and addressing antibiotic resistance. However, AI tools in hiring reveal concerning biases. A recent study found that AI often suggests lower salaries for women and non-white candidates, highlighting the need for human oversight.
