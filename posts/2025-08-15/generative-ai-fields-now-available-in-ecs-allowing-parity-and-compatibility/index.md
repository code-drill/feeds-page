🚀 Exciting updates from Elastic! The Elastic Common Schema (ECS) now supports generative AI (GenAI) fields, enhancing compatibility with OpenTelemetry (OTel). This allows for better normalization of data from diverse GenAI vendors. Key use cases include observability, security, and log normalization for GenAI applications. Users can seamlessly integrate ECS and OTel for improved monitoring. Stay informed and explore how these advancements can benefit your workflows! #Elastic #GenerativeAI...
