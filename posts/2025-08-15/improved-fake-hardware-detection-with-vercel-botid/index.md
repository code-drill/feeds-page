Vercel has enhanced its BotID Deep Analysis with an updated detection model. This improvement expands fingerprinting coverage for headless browsers and simulated device hardware, allowing for better identification of advanced bots. The upgraded model works in real-time without disrupting genuine users. Current users can benefit from these updates immediately by upgrading to botid@1.5.2. 🔍🤖🚀 #BotID #CyberSecurity #TechUpdate
