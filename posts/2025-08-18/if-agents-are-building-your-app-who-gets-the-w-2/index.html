If agents are building your app, who gets the W-2?

2025-08-18 13:00

Autonomous coding agents are changing the software development landscape. 🤖 These agents can now fully design, build, test, and deploy features without human intervention. However, current U.S. GAAP regulations have not fully adapted to this technology. While third-party software costs can be capitalized, developer tools are often classified as overhead. It’s essential to consider how to treat work done by these agents in a manner similar to salaried engineers. #SoftwareDevelopment…
