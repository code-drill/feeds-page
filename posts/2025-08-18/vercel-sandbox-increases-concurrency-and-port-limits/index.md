🚀 Vercel has enhanced its Sandbox capabilities for Pro and Enterprise teams! Now, users can run up to 2,000 Sandboxes concurrently, a significant increase from the previous limit of 150. Each Sandbox can also expose up to 4 ports for external access. This improvement allows for better handling of traffic spikes, untrusted code execution, and more complex applications. For projects needing even higher limits, contacting the sales team is an option. Learn more in the Vercel Sandbox docs! 📊🔧...
