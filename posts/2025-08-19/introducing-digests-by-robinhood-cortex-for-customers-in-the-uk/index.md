🚀 Exciting news for UK investors! Robinhood has launched Digests powered by Robinhood Cortex, an AI-driven feature that explains stock movements in clear language. This tool reviews news, analyst reports, and proprietary data to help users make informed decisions. Available for free on stock detail pages, Digests aims to support investors of all experience levels. For more details, visit the Help Centre. #InvestSmart #StockMarket #AI #Robinhood #UKInvestors
