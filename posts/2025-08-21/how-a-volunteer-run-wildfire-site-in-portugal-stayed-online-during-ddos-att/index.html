How a volunteer-run wildfire site in Portugal stayed online during DDoS attacks

2025-08-21 17:28

🌍🔥 In 2015, fogos.pt began as a volunteer side project to improve access to wildfire data in Portugal. Today, it serves as a vital resource for citizens, media, and government during fire seasons. During the intense 2025 wildfire season, fogos.pt faced DDoS attacks but was protected by Cloudflare’s Project Galileo. This platform provides real-time data and is crucial for public safety.
