Tax and Price Updates for Apps, In-App Purchases, and Subscriptions

2025-08-21 13:00

🌍 The App Store is adjusting prices for apps and in-app purchases due to recent tax and exchange rate changes. 📅 As of August 21, updates include new VAT rates in Estonia and Romania, along with a digital services tax removal in Canada. The Philippines and Vietnam have introduced new VAT and personal income tax rates for developers. 🔄 Starting September 8, pricing updates will occur for apps not using the Philippines or Vietnam as their base storefront.
