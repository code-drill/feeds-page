Cloudflare incident on August 21, 2025

2025-08-22 00:58

On August 21, 2025, Cloudflare experienced significant congestion due to a surge of traffic directed at its clients hosted in AWS us-east-1. 🌐 The congestion began at 16:27 UTC and peaked until 19:38 UTC, affecting user connections with high latency and packet loss. This issue was localized and not a result of an attack. The traffic overload was traced back to a single customer. ⚠️ Cloudflare is working on measures to prevent similar incidents in the future.
