Leveraging technology to transform homeless services

2025-08-25 07:15

Cisco and Destination: Home have partnered to address homelessness in Santa Clara County since 2018. Their initiative focuses on developing supportive housing while leveraging technology to improve access to vital resources for those experiencing homelessness. A recent assessment evaluated the technology needs of the supportive housing system in the area. 🌐🏠🤝 #Homelessness #TechnologyForGood #SupportiveHousing #SantaClara #CommunitySupport
