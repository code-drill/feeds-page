📊 Exciting news for Observability Plus users! Teams can now access 30 days of runtime log retention, which includes valuable details on requests, Vercel Functions, and cache activity. This feature allows you to view, query, and share up to 14 consecutive days of log data at once. Best of all, this extended retention comes at no extra cost for Pro and Enterprise plans! Learn more about Runtime Logs and how to utilize this feature. #ObservabilityPlus #LogRetention #DataInsights
