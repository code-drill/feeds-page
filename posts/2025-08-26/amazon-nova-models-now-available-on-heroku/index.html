Amazon Nova Models: Now Available on Heroku

2025-08-26 15:00

🚀 Exciting news for developers! Amazon’s Nova models are now available on Heroku. Provisioning these advanced AI models is as easy as adding an add-on to your Heroku app. This allows businesses to access powerful, cost-effective AI with simplicity. The Nova family includes nova-pro, designed for accuracy and deep reasoning, and nova-lite, optimized for speed and high-throughput applications. Explore the capabilities of these models today! #AmazonNova #Heroku #AI #MachineLearning #TechNews
