How Ben McCarthy is bringing Obscura’s vision to photo editing

2025-09-01 09:46

📸 Ben McCarthy, creator of Obscura, has launched Obscura Studio, a new photo editing app focused on simplicity and efficiency. The app emerged from the need to adapt to Apple’s Lock Screen Camera extension, prompting a redesign of Obscura. This standalone editor allows for streamlined editing without launching the camera first. McCarthy emphasizes thoughtful design that prioritizes ease of use, aiming to create a product that meets user needs effectively. #PhotoEditing #AppDevelopment…
