Accelerating Stablecoin Innovation in US Banking

2025-09-02 17:00

The stablecoin market is rapidly evolving, with its capitalization exceeding $250 billion recently, and forecasts predict it could reach over $400 billion by 2025 and $2 trillion by 2028. 📈 The U.S. has made strides with the GENIUS Act, the first federal framework for dollar-backed stablecoins, ensuring user protections and transparent operations.
