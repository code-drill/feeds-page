Cisco is committed to enhancing AI education in the U.S. 🌟 As part of the White House's Pledge to America’s Youth, they plan to train 1 million people in AI and digital skills over the next four years via Learn with Cisco. This initiative highlights the importance of preparing individuals for an AI-driven future. Cisco aims to equip educators with the necessary tools and curricula to effectively teach AI. Together, we can empower a new generation for the evolving job market! 💻🤝 #AI #Cisco...
