Is Reactive IT Finally Dead?

2025-09-04 11:23

🚨 Is Reactive IT Finally Dead? 🚨 In a recent article, Cisco’s Jason Coleman highlights the challenges of reactive IT. Engineers often spend over 150 hours a year addressing unexpected outages, leading to high costs and operational risks. Cisco is shifting towards AI-powered, preemptive IT solutions to combat these issues, aiming to minimize downtime and enhance efficiency.
