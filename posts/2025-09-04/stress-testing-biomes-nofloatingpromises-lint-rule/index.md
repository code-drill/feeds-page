We recently collaborated with the Biome team to enhance their noFloatingPromises lint rule. This rule is crucial for identifying unhandled Promises that can lead to errors. To test its effectiveness, we organized a friendly competition to create challenging test cases that could still pass the updated lint rule. This initiative not only fostered creativity but also encouraged learning among our team. Stay tuned for more insights! 🚀💡 #SoftwareDevelopment #LintRules #Teamwork #Innovation...
