🚀 Exciting news for data center operations! Nexus Dashboard 4.1 is now available, offering complete lifecycle management for multiple data center networks through a single interface. This solution simplifies provisioning and enhances visibility while providing diagnostics and automation. It supports various environments, including NX-OS, ACI, AI fabrics, and more. The dashboard also enables VXLAN EVPN automation on Cisco Catalyst switches. #DataCenter #NexusDashboard #AI #NetworkManagement...
