Netlify pricing update: Introducing credit-based plans

2025-09-05 00:17

🚀 Netlify has introduced credit-based plans for new accounts, simplifying pricing for better transparency. Current users can remain on their legacy plans, with options to switch soon. The new system uses a single credit balance instead of multiple metrics. 🔍 Three plans are now available: - Free: 300 credits, $0 - Personal: 1,000 credits, $9/month - Pro: 5,000 credits, $20/member/month Additional credits can be purchased as needed, and costs remain predictable.
